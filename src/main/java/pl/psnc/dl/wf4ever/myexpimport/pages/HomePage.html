In order to start importing your data from myExperiment to dLibra, you need to give have accounts in both services. If you don't have an account in dLibra yet, it will be created automatically for you in step 2 below.

Follow the steps below to give access to the Import Service to your accounts. In steps 1 and 2 you will be redirected to myExperiment and Wf4Ever User Management Service, where you will be asked to log in (if you are not logged in already) and to give authorization.

1. Authorize the Import Service to get your data from myExperiment
2. Authorize the Import Service to put your data to dLibra
3. Import your data
